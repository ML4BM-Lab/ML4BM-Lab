About the Group

Principal Investigator

Mikel Hernaez

The ML4BM Group develops computational methods at the intersection of artificial intelligence and biomedical sciences. Our research spans medical imaging, multi-omics data analysis, and interpretable models for clinical decision support.

We are based at the Center for Applied Medical Research (CIMA), University of Navarra, Pamplona (Spain).

Research Interests

Broad topics driving our current and future research directions.

📷

Medical Image Analysis

Deep learning methods for segmentation, classification, and quantification in radiology and pathology imaging.

🧬

Multi-omics & Genomics

Integrative models combining genomic, transcriptomic, and proteomic data for disease understanding.

🏥

Clinical Decision Support

Predictive models and tools that assist clinicians in diagnosis, prognosis, and treatment planning.

🔍

Interpretable ML

Explainable AI techniques to make biomedical models transparent and trustworthy for clinical adoption.

📈

Biomarker Discovery

Computational pipelines for identifying molecular and imaging biomarkers of disease.

⚙

Reproducible Pipelines

Open, well-documented software workflows for reproducible biomedical data analysis.
